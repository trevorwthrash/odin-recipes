Pickled Rick

This is an old family favorite recipe. It makes a mean pickle.

Ingredients

  • One Rick Sanches
  • Vinegar
  • Water
  • Dill
  • Garlic
  • Various Seasonings

Steps

  1. Whisk together a basic brine of water, vinegar, and seasonings.
  2. Pack the Rick into a jar and add some dill and garlic. Pour the brine over it all.
  3. Refrigerate untill pickled.
